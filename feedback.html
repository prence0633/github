Why We Need Your Feedback

ERDucate was created to make learning Entity-Relationship Diagrams (ERD) and database concepts simple, fun, and effective. To make it even better, we need your honest feedback about your experience. By answering our acceptability questionnaire, you help us improve the lessons, quizzes, and gamified activities to serve you and future students better.

Survey Instructions

Please take a few minutes to answer the questions in our survey. It covers areas such as usability, content clarity, design, and overall acceptability of ERDucate. Your responses will be treated with full confidentiality and will only be used for academic purposes.
