Database & Structured Data Summary

What is Data?
Data refers to raw facts and figures â€” such as names, grades, and contact numbers. On their own, they may not have meaning until processed or organized.

What is a Database?
A database is a digital system that stores organized data in a structured way. It allows users to easily add, view, and update information without confusion or duplication.

Why is Structuring Important?
Structuring ensures data is arranged properly â€” using tables, fields, and relationships â€” so it remains accurate, consistent, and easy to analyze. This process helps organizations avoid errors and make data-driven decisions effectively.
